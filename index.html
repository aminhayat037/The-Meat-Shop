About Us

At “The Meat Shop” East Village, we are an independent run butcher committed to providing our customers with the highest quality 100% Halal meats. Whether you’re looking for everyday essentials, family favourites, or specialty cuts for a special occasion, our experienced team is here to help. Customer service is at the heart of everything we do. We believe in offering honest advice, exceptional quality, and great value to the local community we proudly serve. Our commitment is simple: freshness, quality and trust.
